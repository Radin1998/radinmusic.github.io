Radin crafts music that moves both body and mind, drawing on rhythms from across the globe. Born in Sweden with Iranian heritage, his multicultural background shapes a distinctive, immersive sound.

His sets are hypnotic, fluid, and deeply movement-driven, designed to captivate, connect, and transport. Every performance becomes a journey, turning the dancefloor into a space where sound and culture meet.
